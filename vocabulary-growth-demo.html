Interactive · Study 01

Vocabulary growth, carrying its own uncertainty

The posterior from the pooled Down-syndrome vocabulary model — how many words children understand, say and sign across age, each as a credible range rather than a single line. Drag through age, and use the dispersion control to see why the spread itself is the scientific question.

Curves and shaded bands are the study's real posterior (VG14 development-tier fit; findings preliminary). The dispersion control is an illustrative what-if that rescales the credible bands to show their sensitivity to the over-dispersion κ — it does not re-fit the model.
